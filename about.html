About Me

The mind is not a vessel to be filled but a fire to be kindled - Plutarch

Derek Stampone was born and raised in Orange Country, New York where he graduated with an International Baccalaurate Diploma from Pine Bush High School.

Derek continued his studies at SUNY Geneseo where he earned his B.A. in Physics while working at an Undergraduate Lab Instructor and a Resistant Advisor.

After a stint at Binghamton University where he earned his M.S. in Physics, Derek moved to New York City to become a High School Physics teacher as a New York City Teaching Fellow.

Over the past four years, Derek has grown the Physics program at the High School of Fashion Industries and improved his pedagogy as an Eary Career Fellow at Math for America.

Derek is most passionate about disrupting the inequitable access of Physics (and computer science) in New York City. He is a member of his school's Equity Team where he plies his analytical skills as Data Specialist to guide the school through the RIDES Institute at Harvard

When not teaching, Derek enjoys cooking, reading, playing guitar, and working out at CrossFit NYC.
